Application to test browser user-input timing performance.

Version 0.1.0 -- Joris Derese

The browser will register the time between pairs of consecutive keystrokes. Execute pairs of two keystrokes to see the results.

The browser will also register the time spent by the mouse pointer inside the blue box. To start testing, move your mouse pointer in and out the blue box below:

Press the 'Generate results arrays' button to display the results as CSV strings.
